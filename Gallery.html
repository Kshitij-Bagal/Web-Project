    Butea-Monospera

    Butea monosperma is a species of Butea native to tropical and sub-tropical parts of South Asia and Southeast Asia, ranging across Bangladesh, India, Nepal, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, Myanmar, Thailand, Laos, Cambodia, Vietnam, Malaysia, and western Indonesia.

    • Subfamily: Faboideae
    • Order: Fabales

    Deloxin-Regia

    Delonix regia is a species of flowering plant in the bean family Fabaceae, subfamily Caesalpinioideae native to Madagascar. It is noted for its fern-like leaves and flamboyant display of orange-red flowers over summer.

    • Scientific name: Delonix regia
    • Family: Fabaceae
    • Subfamily: Caesalpinioideae
    • Order: Fabales

    Fox-Brush-Orchid

    Aerides, known commonly as cat's-tail orchids and fox brush orchids, is a genus belonging to the orchid family. It is a group of tropical epiphyte orchids that grow mainly in the warm lowlands of tropical Asia from India to southern China to New Guinea.

    • Scientific name: Aerides
    • Family: Orchidaceae
    • Order: Asparagales
    • Kingdom: Plantae

    Hibiscus

    Hibiscus is a genus of flowering plants in the mallow family, Malvaceae. The genus is quite large, comprising several hundred species that are native to warm temperate, subtropical and tropical regions throughout the world.

    • Scientific name: Hibiscus
    • Family: Malvaceae
    • Order: Malvales

    Marigold

    Tagetes is a genus of 50 species of annual or perennial, mostly herbaceous plants in the family Asteraceae. They are among several groups of plants known in English as marigolds. The genus Tagetes was described by Carl Linnaeus in 1753.

    • Scientific name: Tagetes
    • Family: Asteraceae
    • Order: Daisy family
    • Kingdom: Plantae
    Favourite

    Pansy

    The garden pansy is a type of polychromatic large-flowered hybrid plant cultivated as a garden flower. It is derived by hybridization from several species in the section Melanium of the genus Viola, particularly V. tricolor, a wildflower of Europe and western Asia known as heartsease.

    • Scientific name: Viola tricolor var. hortensis
    • Family: Violaceae
    • Order: Malpighiales
    • Kingdom: Plantae
    Favourite

    Zinnia

    Zinnia is a genus of plants of the tribe Heliantheae within the family Asteraceae. They are native to scrub and dry grassland in an area stretching from the Southwestern United States to South America, with a centre of diversity in Mexico.

    • Scientific name: Zinnia
    • Family: Asteraceae
    • Subfamily: Asteroideae
    • Order: Asterales
    • Kingdom: Plantae
    • Tribe: Heliantheae
